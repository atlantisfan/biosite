Fun with Power Tools

Matt enjoys working with his hands. Hobbies that involve physical movement are essential. Matt has always had a deep creative side that expressed in drawing, graphic design, photography, and wood working. His introduction to the CNC Machine opened a completely new avenue to work creatively and make a little extra cash.

From Placards to signs, to customized iPhone cases, Matt creates marvels that have become gifts for friends and purchases for the world on Etsy.
