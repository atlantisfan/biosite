The Coach who would be a teacher

Matthew Evens has worked as a teacher for nearly for more than 7 years in the private sector, instructing teenagers in subjects such as Health, Physical Education, Computer Science and programming, and religeous studies.

Matthew Evens started his career path to coaching at Union College in Lincoln, Nebraska where he studied Physical Education, Computer Science and Mathematics. He has used every skill gained in his education and much more through his work as a camp counselor, assistant coaching positions, computer software development and personal love for the topics he teaches.

He has overcome several challenges in both his personal life and career and continues to strive to give his upmost to his students and his family. Matthew currently resides in Lumberton Mississippi, where he and his wife are caring for their recently born son.

Matthew Evens is inspirational to his colleagues and friends for his dedication to whatever he's called upon to do. His love for guiding and helping people of all ages to become better human beings through hard work and persistence has gained him a special affection from his students. When he isn't coaching, he's teaching. When he isn't guiding, he's instructing through play.
