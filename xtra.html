Fun with Power Tools

Matt enjoys working with his hands. Hobbies that involve physical movement are essential. Matt has always had a deep creative side that expressed in drawing, graphic design, photography, and wood working.

What started out as a hobby has grown into a small business and means to channel his creativity and love for design using a CNC machine. A CNC machine is a Computer Numerical Controlled device that removes layers of material from a stock piece. Matt has used his creativity to generate placks, commemorative signs and art pieces with the CNC machine. Even creating a large sliding barn door for to cover an open hallway in his home. Today he continues to work with new materials to create more exotic artistic masterpieces.

From Placards to signs, to customized iPhone cases, Matt creates marvels that have become gifts for friends and purchases for the world on Etsy.

Matthew Evens is inspirational to his colleagues and friends for his dedication to whatever he's called upon to do. His love for guiding and helping people of all ages to become better human beings through hard work and persistence has gained him a special affection form his students. When he isn't coaching, he's teaching. When he isn't guiding, he's instructing through play.
